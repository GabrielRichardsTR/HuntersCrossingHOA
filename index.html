Our Neighborhood

A community worth
coming home to

Hunters Crossing is a small, informal homeowner community in Ann Arbor. We don't have a lot of rules — just neighbors who care about keeping our street a great place to live.

The HOA exists to coordinate the things that benefit everyone — shared maintenance, community communication, and making sure new neighbors feel welcome.

Have a question about the neighborhood? The board is here to help. Reach us anytime at hunterstrailannarbor@gmail.com.

Helpful Information

What neighbors
ask about most

🗑
Trash & Recycling
Trash pickup is every Monday.
Recycling is every other Monday.
Bins should be out by 7am and returned by end of day.
🏡
Property Changes
Planning a fence, shed, or exterior modification? Email the board before starting so we can make sure everything goes smoothly with the city.
💰
HOA Dues
Annual dues help cover shared neighborhood costs. Questions about your balance or payment? Contact the board treasurer directly.
🚗
Parking
Please keep commercial vehicles and long-term street parking to a minimum out of courtesy for your neighbors.
🌿
Lawn & Landscaping
Keeping lawns tidy during the growing season helps maintain property values for everyone. The board appreciates your effort.
🆘
Emergencies
For any emergency, call 911.
For non-urgent neighborhood issues, email the board and we'll follow up promptly.
